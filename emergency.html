Emergency Response

For any emergency, you may dial 112 and provide the required information.Regular training programs will eb conducted and flyers will be distributed to increase the awareness of halndling emergency situations. You are required to acts as per the instructions as we give first priopity for the welbeing of human lives.

Fire Safety

In case of fire alarm activation, you are required to leave the premises immediately together with any clients inside the office. Fire Rescue assistance button is at the main door and you may press the same in case of visibility of fire or flame.

Burglary and Security

Team members are advised to act vigilantly. In case of a burglary, the panic button for police alarm link is available on the desk. All emergency contacts are available on office mobile phones and are automatically updated on a regular basis. You may communicate all emergency situations to the 'Emergency' WhatsApp group on office mobiles.

Parking and Security

For the convenience, parking lots are reserved for staff and customers. Customer and staff parking slots are available in front of the premises, and the company is not liable for any losses. Security personnel are not available in the parking area.
