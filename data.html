Data Protection

This is a significantly improtant espect of all businesses and this will All information related to customers is required to be considered as sensitive and confidential. According to the Privacy Policy of the company, you are not allowed to disclose any information to a third party under any circumstances, including Paytrails and Pixkeys.

Data Transfer

Customer details will be transfered to Pixkeys and Paytrails for several transactions. Selected information of customers, will be transfered and such data transfer within business partners will be done through secured transfer protocols to adhere to regulatory and compliance requirements such as encyption.Strong authentication methods are inplace to verify the sender/receiver.

User Logging and Access

Unique User ID and Password is assigned to each team member. Password related matters will be guided according to Password Policy of the company. Three unsuccessful / incorrect password entries will lock the users. You may contact the adminitrator to unlock/reset the same.User logging and access to data will be monitored, and logs will be maintained. Suspicious activities or unauthorized access attempts will be prevented, and investigations will be followed by the administrator.

Duplicate Ticket Issuance and Refunds

Customers are entilled to request refunds by providing original ticket. In case of refunds, team members are not allowed to request details related to customers' card numbers or account numbers. All such reversals will be raised through the unique reference number issued to the customer at the time of ticket issuance.Duplicate ticket issuance will be done after obtaining proper written request and voiding previous ticket with the unique reference number.

Privacy Policy Compliance

As customer information are high sensitive information, company is required to handle such information with grater care. Any missuse of information will lead reputation loss of the company. All matters will be handled subject to the Privacy Policy, such as Marketing and Promotions, and the publication of customer reviews. Data breach response plan is the duty of the administrator.

Database Backup

Database backup is scheduled on a daily basis, and backup servers are located in multiple locations.Business Continuity Plan will be followed on predefined date to test the backups.
